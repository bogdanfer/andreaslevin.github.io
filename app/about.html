About

I'm a maker of all things

Profile

Born in Sweden, I ventured to London in my early twenties to work at AKQA as my first step into advertising. With four years of agency experience of working on mainly Nike, I moved to Amsterdam to broaden my experience. I've since then had the chance to build upon my graphic design knowledge by defining art direction, shaping user epxeriences and leading smaller teams.

Most recently, I've designed and developed the new KLM Kiosk app, as well as art directed and lead the design of two major bicycle sites.
