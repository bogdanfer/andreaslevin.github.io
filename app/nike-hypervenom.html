Nike Hypervenom

Design Lead Digital

A new breed of attack

2 weeks, 2013
Nike - Football

Hypervenom is the choice of Neymar Jr. The one boot made for the attacking goal scorer, all while looking extremely haunting to anyone standing in its way. Nike Hypervenom is one of Nike Football’s most technically advanced boot.

The Hypervenom site elevates the boot through an interactive and animated campaign experience. Using the artwork from Nike, and pushing it one step further, the user can learn about the boot in it’s true shape and form.

Campaign

The campaign experience promotes the Hypervenom boot and it’s advanced innovation through the “Monsters” that make up “A new breed of attack”.

Artwork

The “Monsters” brand assets from Nike inspired the layout and look and feel of the digital experience of the campaign.

Messaging

Emails, social posts and many more assets were designed to support the messaging behind the campaign.

Elements

For a consistent look and feel, a design library was crafted to provide assets for all creative throughout the campaign.

Discovery

By featuring an iconographic overview filter of each style, and the combination of lifestyle and product photography the user is guided to the right choice.
